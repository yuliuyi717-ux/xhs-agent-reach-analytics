# Takedown Policy (Template)

## 1. Purpose

This policy provides a channel for reporting alleged copyright, privacy, or unlawful-content issues related to project outputs.

## 2. Report Channel

Send reports to: `[COMPLIANCE_EMAIL]`

## 3. Required Information

Please include:

- Your name and contact information
- Description of the material at issue
- Exact location (file path, commit hash, URL)
- Legal basis of the request (copyright, privacy, other)
- Statement of good-faith belief and accuracy

## 4. Handling Process

- Acknowledge receipt within `[ACK_DAYS]` business days
- Initial review within `[REVIEW_DAYS]` business days
- If valid, remove/disable access in reasonable time
- Notify relevant parties when appropriate

## 5. Counter-Notice

If a report is disputed, maintainers may request additional evidence and process a counter-notice workflow consistent with local law.

## 6. Repeat Abuse

Repeated malicious reports or repeated infringing use may result in support refusal or repository governance actions.
